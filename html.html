ABOUT

A hobby is something which one loves to do in his leisure time. Different people have different hobbies. My favorite hobby is reading books. I spend my leisure time reading all types of books. Literature is my favorite genre. I spend most of my time in reading novels, drama and short stories. I have made a collection of several books. I have books of Shakespeare, Charles Dickens, Panchtantra, Story of Prophets, etc. Books make me wise and keep me away from bad company. I love books so much that I often forget to take my food on time. I am proud of my hobby.
